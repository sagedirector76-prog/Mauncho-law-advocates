About Our Firm

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Mauncho Advocates is a boutique law firm led by Mauncho Edwin, an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. With a diverse range of expertise covering several practice areas, our team is committed to delivering exceptional legal services that exceed expectations.

Based in Nanyuki Town at Laipha House, we serve clients across Kenya with professionalism and dedication. We pride ourselves on understanding our clients' needs and providing tailored legal solutions that achieve outstanding results.
